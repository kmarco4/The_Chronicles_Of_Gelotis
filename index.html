Chapter 1

I woke up shivering in a strange, dark room I`ve never been before. There were tubes going down my throat.

Where am I? How did I get here?

I hear a woman scream; he is awake. It was so loud my ears started bleeding. I can`t move.

Dear God, am I paralyzed? What have these people done to me?

As she exits the room, the lights from outside burn my eyes and leaves me blind. I feel myself starting to lose consciousness.

Who am I? What is my name?
