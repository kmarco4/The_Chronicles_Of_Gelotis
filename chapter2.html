Chapter 2

I am awake now, floating in a tank of salt water. I see a man, typing on a computer that is connected to the tank. I begin to hear voices in my head.

"Good morning, Gelotis"

"I know this is a lot to take in right now, but you are a lucky man. You should be dead right now, but you were hand picked by the master to be saved and to continue our legacy."

What is he talking about?

"He picked you because of the rare gift you were born with. You have a rare dormant gene that allows you to supress all five senses that makes you have a faster reaction time and heightened sensitivity in the sense you choose."

"We activated this gift and you must learn to control it. We will guide you to your destiny of becoming our new master."

Towards the end, I began to recognize the voice. It was my mother's. She killed herself by swallowing pills as I watched, when I was 13 years old.

My memory is coming back to me. My life is awful. I wish they hadn't saved me.

I began hitting my head against the glass of the tank. I can see it begin to fill with blood.

My mother yells at me.

"Stop!"

The tank begins to shake and I feel myself unable to move.

I hope I am dying.
